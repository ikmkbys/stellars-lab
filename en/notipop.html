Getting Started with Notipop

From install to your first Notion save — set up in under 5 minutes.

1

Install the Chrome extension

Get Notipop from the Chrome Web Store.

📦 Get it on Chrome Web Store (coming soon) 🔧 Chrome Web Store submission is in progress
2

Connect to Notion

Open the Notipop popup and click "Connect to Notion".
Authorize the connection in the Notion screen that appears.

[ Notipop popup → "Connect to Notion" button ]
3

Choose your Notion database

After connecting, select the Notion database where your notes will be saved.
You can change this anytime from the extension's options page.

[ Database selection screen ]
4

Select text and save

Highlight any text on a webpage, open the Notipop popup, and click "Save to Notion".
The page title and URL are attached automatically.

Use the shortcut Ctrl + Shift + M to open the popup instantly.

[ Select text → popup → saved to Notion ]

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

?

Does it work with the free Notion plan?

Yes. Notipop works with any Notion plan, including the free tier.

?

Where does my data go?

Text is saved directly to your own Notion database.
Stellars Lab does not store any of your content.

?

What formatting is supported?

Plain text, headings (H1–H4), and bold text. The structure of your selection is preserved when sent to Notion.
